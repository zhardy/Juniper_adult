Reading Series





Each night during the Juniper Institute, acclaimed faculty and writers in residence read from their new and selected work. Readings are free and open to the public. There is plenty of free parking and the event is in an accessible venue:

UMass Amherst Integrated Sciences Building, Room 135, 661 North Pleasant Street, Amherst MA

Readings begin each night at 7:30 and are followed by book signings.
